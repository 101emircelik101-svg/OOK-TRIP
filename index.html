Trip Overview

Mission: The privilege of international travel comes with the responsibility to engage mindfully and ethically. This proposal outlines a comprehensive cultural and historical immersion into Bodrum, Turkey. By stepping away from our normal routines, students will find opportunities for shared conversation, historical education, and well-being.

Attractions & Experiences

Our itinerary balances ancient history with natural beauty and local culture:

Historical Sites

  • Bodrum Castle & Underwater Archaeology: Exploring the fortress built from the stones of the Mausoleum and discovering ancient shipwrecks.
  • Mausoleum at Halicarnassus: Visiting the ruins of one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.
  • Bodrum Theater: An ancient amphitheater overlooking the city.
  • Bodrum Windmills: Historic stone windmills with panoramic views.

Culture & Nature

  • Orak Island: A boat trip to experience the pristine waters and unique geography of the region.
  • Zeki Müren Arts Museum: Celebrating one of Turkey's most famous musicians and actors.
  • Yalikavak Marina: Exploring modern retail and observing local commerce.
